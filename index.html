The Chaos Returns.

Welcome to Unstable Universe — a realm where strategy, betrayal, and alliances decide the fate of every block.

Unstable SMP is a hardcore minecraft server where you are given only one life, in which 4 main characters (Parrot, Spoke, Wemmbu and Flame) go on their own adventures throughout the world, dealing with different enemies, making allies and telling their own story.

The series deals with the takeover of the mafia, a group that always wears invisibility. This group follows the rule of a dictator named Ashwagg, and from the teamwork of the main characters, Ash is dethroned. After this, they all go their separate ways. However, everyone starts struggling with a faction on the server called the LAW. This group promotes justice on the server, when in reality they’re manipulating everyone to believe their story.

Watch the most intense SMP on YouTube. New episodes weekly.
